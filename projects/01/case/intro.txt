At 2AM, the world quiets, and emotions surface unfiltered. It is both the night’s deepest hush and the moment when thoughts emerge raw and vulnerable. Texting at 2AM is an act of honesty—love, longing, and hesitation—all laid bare in messages we send (or don’t).  The Oslo-based artist duo 2AM-DM captures this fleeting moment and transforms it into a powerful artistic expression. In collaboration with Sony Music Norway, we translated this raw vulnerability into typography.
