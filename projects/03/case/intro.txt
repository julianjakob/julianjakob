Makhulo’s vision goes beyond music. It’s about moving culture forward. His lyrics aren’t just catchy, they’re considered, carrying messages that stay with you and encourage a more positive direction.

To reflect this, I moved away from static, conventional type and created a custom lettering system built around connection. Each letterform is hand-crafted, designed to link, interact and evolve as part of a larger whole.

The identity becomes a visual extension of Makhulo’s voice—one that’s rooted in exchange, transformation, and the idea that small connections can lead to something bigger.
