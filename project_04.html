UNTOLD MUSIC FESTIVAL

Untold Music Festival, held annually in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, is one of Europe's premier electronic music events. Since its 2015 debut, it has featured top DJs like Armin van Buuren and David Guetta. Known for its impressive stage designs and vibrant atmosphere, Untold attracts hundreds of thousands of fans from around the world, making it a must-visit for electronic music lovers. I had the opportunity to work on Untold Music Festival for my bachelor project, where I've developed a dynamic visual system to enhance Untold's positioning on the festival market. Over one month, I've crafted this adaptable visual framework and created a geo-node system that visualizes music, seamlessly integrating with the visual system to deliver an immersive experience.
